GOGO

User Experience Design

In 12 hours, I and a group of 6 others created a revamp app, GoGo, for SLO Safe Ride at Camp PolyHacks. Our team placed third out of fifteen teams. We wanted to create an application that didn't associate itself with the previous SLO Safe Ride app. Our goal was to create an easy to use app that would help decrease drunk driving.

The biggest advantange to our app was a quick ride button. The quick ride button allowed people to download the app without having to instantly create a login. The button is directed for those who may be too intoxicated or wanted to use the app once. To make the app experience simple we implemented a credit card reader that could easily take a photo of a debit/credit card rather than making the customer manually enter their information.

We felt that SLO Safe Ride offered really great rates and rewards, people just weren't unaware. Therefore, we decided to include a rewards system to give people an incenstive to use the app over competitors.
